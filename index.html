About
What is HatzCatz?
🐱🎩

The Story

HatzCatz is a collection of 2222 unique pixel art NFTs living on the Base network. Each HatzCatz is a one-of-a-kind character featuring a different hat, fur, expression, and accessory — no two are alike. Some cats wear hats. HatzCatz wear identity.

🎨

Pixel Art

Every HatzCatz is hand-crafted in pixel art style, generated from hundreds of unique traits to create truly one-of-a-kind digital identities.

⬡

Built on Base

HatzCatz lives on Base — Ethereum's fastest and most developer-friendly L2. Low gas fees mean more minting, less waiting.
